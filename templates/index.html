Online Office Hours

Below is a list of mentor availability. Students who need online office hours should send me an email at jalal.maaouni@um6p.ma with:

  • What you want to go over during the office hour (topics / problems / code).
  • Which two slots below work best for you.

Available slots

  • Wednesday from 3 to 5
  • Thursday from 9 to 12
  • Thursday from 3 to 5
  • Friday from 2 to 6

Office hours guidelines

  • Office hours are not a replacement for the lecture or for your own study time.
  • You should have already studied the material and tried the exercises on your own.
  • You should have already tried the review quizzes on your own.
  • Each slot is about 15–20 minutes, so come with specific questions, not “explain the whole chapter”.
  • Have your code, notes, and questions ready at the start; do not use the slot to rediscover where the problem is.
  • Show your attempts and thought process; mentors expect to see what you already tried.
  • Use the time to clarify blocking points and gain maximum value, not to start from zero.
